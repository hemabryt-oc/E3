Who We Are

E³ Technologies — Encouraging Excellence Early

E³ is Africa's AI-powered technology builder. We create custom websites, mobile apps, and intelligent systems that solve real problems for real businesses — starting in Nigeria, growing across the continent.

We were founded by Bright Ameh on a simple belief: that a teenager in a village with only a smartphone should be capable of learning, building, and launching a global digital business.

See What We Build →
"Africa has brilliant founders and exceptional ambition. What it needs is world-class digital infrastructure to match."

That belief is our mission. Every website, every app, every AI system we build is one step closer to an Africa where every business — regardless of size or location — has the digital tools to compete globally.

Founder & CEO

Bright Ameh

Founder & CEO, E³ Technologies

Bright founded E³ with the conviction that Africa's next generation of businesses, founders, and innovators deserve the same quality of technology as any company anywhere in the world.

His vision extends beyond building websites. E³ is building an ecosystem — Academy, Nurse AI, Mercury, Verify — each one a response to a real problem facing African people and businesses today.
